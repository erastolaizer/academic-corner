OUR MISSION AND VISSION Mission
To inspire and impower students by engaging them to learn various Subjects through E-learning.

Vision
Education has its own unique heritage in its own context. Academic corner aim to prepare our Tanzanians to cope with rapidly changing world by being able to understand what they are taught and learn in various language, and be able to eplain them in our Swahili language. So as to help them to apply directly what learned in English to Tanzanian swahili speakers. Hence growth of our Swahili language.
